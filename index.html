Awards

Jagattarini Gold Medal 1945 From the University of Calcutta for his work in Bengali Literature.
Padma Bhushan 1960 One of the highest civilian honors of the Republic of India.
National Poet
Conferred the title of Government of Bangladesh.
Ekushey Padak 1976 Bangladesh's highest civilian honours.
